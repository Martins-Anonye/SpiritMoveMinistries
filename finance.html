Finance is the key point of expansion. Finance is what trigger action in welfare,  services and help to increase passion.  Jesus feed the crowed due to his financial capability.  We do not ask big or small. Just do as you are being read.

God has given us, supporting the ministerial work of God is another way of appreciating God.    
Giving generously and sacrificially from our finances is countercultural, but believers should want to follow Christ, not the world’s example.
Using our money to support  missionary work to unreached people groups, gospel-centered help for those in poverty, and causes of justice are all ways we can lay up treasure in Heaven.
