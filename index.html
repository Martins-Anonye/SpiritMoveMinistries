SPIRIT MOVEMENT MINISTRIES 

Motto: Christ the Power of Age to Come

Blessing Ume is a woman of God with fire and love of the Lord Jesus in her bones. Serving from the State of New Jersey where she's based, She has been ministering unto the Lord through preaching, zoom meeting, healings and prophetic ministration. Through demostration and manifestation of the power of Holy Spirit, she bring Christ for salvation and strenthening of souls.
Blessing encountered God at an early stage of her life in Africa through the revelation of the cross in a powerful way. Having received heart for the burden of the Lord Jesus during this encounter, she became passionate for souls and dedicated to the work of God. 

She is the founder of the Spirit Move Ministries with Apostolic and prophetic mandate for gospel and revival, and for the out pouring of the Holy Spirit in this end time. Preparing  the way for the second coming of the  Lord Jesus Christ, I will then have a reason to say "come Lord Jesus, come".


Spirit Move  is a non-denominational / inter-denominational Christian Movement with a mandate to preach the gospel and bring healing. Through programs and conferences designed to bring about unity in the body of Christ, We bring King Jesus via Revival crusade, Healing crusade, and Holy Spirit Move conferences. We preach the resurrected Christ and His coming back, we sow into raising of disciples of all nations for the Great Commission of the Lord Jesus in taking the gospel to the ends of the earth.
