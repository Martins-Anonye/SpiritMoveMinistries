Welfare Ministry

Love is the key to Gods kingdom. Galatians 5:22 : But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness.

Romans 13:8 --> Owe no one anything, except to love each other, for the one who loves another has fulfilled the law.
